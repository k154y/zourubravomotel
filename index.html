Welcome to ZOURU BRAVO MOTEL

Your Lakeside Escape in Rubavu

Nestled on the serene shores of Rubavu Artificial Forest, ZOURU BRAVO MOTEL offers you a peaceful getaway where nature, comfort, and Rwandan hospitality come together. Whether you're here for relaxation, adventure, or business, our cozy accommodations and breathtaking views are here to make your stay unforgettable.

Come in as a guest, leave as family.

Accommodation

At ZOURU BRAVO MOTEL, our rooms are thoughtfully designed to provide comfort, privacy, and a touch of elegance. Whether you're traveling solo, as a couple, or with family, we offer a variety of room options to suit your needs — from cozy single rooms to spacious apartment. Each room features modern amenities, comfortable bedding, and a peaceful ambiance to help you unwind after a day of exploring Rubavu.

Wake up to the gentle sounds of Rubavu Artificial forest and experience true rest in a space that feels like home.

Bar & Restaurant

Savor the taste of Rwanda and beyond at our Bar & Restaurant, where every meal is crafted with care and flavor. From freshly prepared local dishes to international favorites, our menu offers something for every palate. Enjoy your meal in a relaxed atmosphere with stunning views of Rubacu Artificial Forest, and don't miss our selection of refreshing drinks, cocktails, and local beverages at the bar.

Whether it's breakfast by the lake, a sunset dinner, or a casual drink with friends, ZOURU BRAVO MOTEL offers a dining experience to remember.

Apartment Facilities

Welcome to our modern and fully furnished apartments, designed for comfort, convenience, and style. Each unit features spacious living areas, well-equipped kitchens, cozy bedrooms, and private balconies with stunning views. Whether you're staying short-term or long-term, our apartments offer a peaceful retreat with easy access to nearby shops, restaurants. Enjoy secure parking, high-speed Wi-Fi, and a welcoming environment your perfect home away from home in Rubavu.
